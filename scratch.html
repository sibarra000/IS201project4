What is Lacrosse?

Lacrosse is described as "the fastest sport on two feet". It is played by passing a rubber ball between players using a stick with the intent of scoring in the opposing team's net. Many say that it has many attributes similar to other sports like basketball, football, soccer, and hockey.

Here are some interesting rules:

  1. Players can move behind the goal similar to hockey.
  2. On a missed shot, the team with the player closest to the endline when the ball crosses gets posession.
  3. Each posession is started by a faceoff, where two players flip their lacrosse heads and try to get the ball by bending the head of the stick around it or by pushing it out.
